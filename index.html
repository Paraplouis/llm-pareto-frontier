How to read this chart?

The cost of LLMs is steadily falling, and their quality is rising. This chart helps you navigate the trade-offs.

Quality (Elo Score)

We use the ELO score (with style control) from the official LM Arena leaderboard dataset as a proxy for quality. It's like a chess Elo rating, but for LLMs, based on thousands of human votes.

Cost ($/MTok)

The price is calculated from both input and output token rates (per million) based on the prompt/output split you choose above (default 3:1). Pricing data comes from the OpenRouter models API. Historically this chart used input-only pricing as a proxy for cost—equivalent to a 1:0 ratio—which you can still reproduce via the preset buttons.
